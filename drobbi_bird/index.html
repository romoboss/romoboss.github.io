Drobbi Bird

Drobbi Bird is a tap-scroller video game. The game's core mechanics were based on the Game Maker's Toolkit's "The Unity Tutorial For Complete Beginners" on YouTube. Drobbi Bird draws inspiration from the classic game Flappy Bird. Players control a character equipped with a jetpack, tapping the screen to propel the character upward. The objective is to navigate through an endless series of pipes without colliding with them.
