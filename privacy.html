Privacy Policy for Romoboss Games and Website

Effective Date: 13th December 2024

Romoboss Games (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the website romoboss.github.io and hosts games, including Drobbi Bird. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or play our games.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Website (romoboss.github.io)

We do not directly collect personal information on our website. However, third-party services integrated into the site may collect data as per their respective privacy policies. These services include:

    Embedded Content: Links to external platforms such as Itch.io, YouTube, and Discord.

    Cookies: Third-party services may use cookies for analytics or embedded content.

1.2 Games (e.g., Drobbi Bird)

When playing our games, the following data may be collected and stored:

    Game Progress: Saved locally in your browser using Unity’s PlayerPrefs, via IndexedDB.

    Leaderboard Data: High scores and chosen usernames are submitted to the leaderboard system hosted by Leaderboard Creator.

2. How We Use Collected Information

2.1 Website Usage

Data collected by third-party services on the website (e.g., through embedded YouTube videos or links) is used as per their privacy policies. We do not process or store website user data ourselves.

2.2 Game Data

    Local Game Data: Data stored using PlayerPrefs (e.g., high scores, settings) is used to enhance gameplay and improve the user experience. This data remains on your device unless cleared manually by the user.

    Leaderboard Submissions: High scores and usernames submitted to the leaderboard are displayed publicly to create a competitive and engaging environment.

3. How We Share Information

We do not directly share or sell your data. However:

    Data submitted to Leaderboard Creator (high scores and usernames) is processed and stored by their system. Please refer to Leaderboard Creator’s Privacy Policy for more details.

    Third-party services embedded on our website or games (e.g., YouTube, Discord, Itch.io) may collect data independently.

4. Data Retention

4.1 Local Game Data

Data saved via PlayerPrefs is retained indefinitely on your browser unless you clear your browser cache or delete the data manually.

4.2 Leaderboard Data

Leaderboard submissions are retained indefinitely but can be removed upon user request (see Section 6).

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website and games do not use cookies directly. However, third-party services linked or embedded in our website may use cookies or tracking technologies. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

6. Your Data Rights

6.1 Leaderboard Data

You have the right to:

    Request removal of your leaderboard entry. Press the “Remove my data from the leaderboard” button or contact us directly via one of the contact options listed at romoboss.github.io/aboutme.

    Request information about the data we hold related to your leaderboard submission.

    For more information on how you can delete your data from the leaderboard in Drobbi Bird, read romoboss.github.io/games/drobbi-bird/data-deletion.

6.2 General Rights

If you are in the EU (under GDPR) or California (under CCPA), you may have additional rights, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

7. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. However, data transmitted to third-party systems (e.g., Leaderboard Creator) is subject to their security measures. Please review their policies for details.

8. Third-Party Services

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services we link to or use. We encourage you to review their policies:

    Leaderboard Creator Privacy Policy

    Itch.io Privacy Policy

    YouTube Privacy Policy

    Discord Privacy Policy

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and games are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately. If you believe your child has submitted data, please contact us via one of the contact options listed at romoboss.github.io/aboutme.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and the “Effective Date” will be updated accordingly.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via one of the contact options listed at romoboss.github.io/aboutme.
