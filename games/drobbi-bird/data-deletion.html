Delete your data from Drobbi Bird

Leaderboard Data

You have the right to:

    Request removal of your leaderboard entry. Press the “Remove my data from the leaderboard” button or contact us directly via one of the contact options listed at romoboss.github.io/aboutme.

    Request information about the data we hold related to your leaderboard submission.

Steps on how to delete your data from the leaderboard

    1. Inside of the game:

      1. Launch "Drobbi Bird" using the device on which you submitted the data that you want to get removed from the servers.

      2. While in the main menu, click on the button that leads you to the leaderboards section (orange button with three black colored figures drawn on it).

      3. Click on the "Remove my data from the leaderboard" button.

      4. You're all done, all of the data submitted from the device you are currently using is deleted from the servers!

    2. By contacting us outside the app (will take longer):

      Contact us directly via one of the contact options listed at romoboss.github.io/aboutme.

      Make sure to provide your username and proof that you own the account that submitted the score.

      I will try to respond as soon as possible. In case I do not reply to your message and the data still remains on the leaderboard, try to contact me using a different method.

      The proccess of manual removal may take up to 30 days.
